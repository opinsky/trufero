The Illiquid Asset Reporting Bottleneck is Over.

General Partners report in wildly different formats, burying critical performance data inside unstructured PDFs, capital call notices, and K-1s. Trufero changes the paradigm.

1. Intelligent Ingestion

Simply forward GP emails or drop PDFs into Trufero. Our document-AI engine natively understands unstructured financial layouts, extracting specific line items without manual data entry templates.

2. Automated Standardization

We normalize distinct reporting methodologies into a single, cohesive taxonomy. A capital account statement from Sequoia is seamlessly mapped alongside a quarterly update from KKR.

3. Real-Time Reconciliation

The system automatically reconciles called capital against commitments, tracks unfunded liabilities, and flags discrepancies in GP reported fees and carry calculations.
